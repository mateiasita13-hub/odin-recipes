Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar salad is a classic salad made with romaine lettuce, croutons, parmesan cheese, and a creamy Caesar dressing. It is fresh, crunchy, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce
  2. Prepare croutons by toasting bread cubes in the oven
  3. Grate parmesan cheese
  4. Mix lettuce with Caesar dressing
  5. Add croutons and parmesan on top
  6. Squeeze lemon juice and add black pepper
