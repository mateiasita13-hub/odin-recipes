Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most popular dishes in the world. It consists of a round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, baked in a hot oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees Celsius
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and add basil leaves
  6. Bake for 15 minutes until crust is golden
