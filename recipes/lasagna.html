Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel, and melted cheese. It is baked in the oven until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius
  2. Cook the pasta sheets in boiling water
  3. Brown the ground beef in a pan with olive oil
  4. Add tomato sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Layer pasta, meat sauce, and bechamel in a baking dish
  6. Top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  7. Bake for 30 minutes until golden
